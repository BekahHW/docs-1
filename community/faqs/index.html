Frequently asked Questions

  1. How do I find good first issues? We believe that good first issues don't exist, and the best way to find an issue to work on is to create the issue yourself.

    However, sites like good first issues and first timers only can lead you to projects and issues worth working on. You can also use the good first issue label in the search engine of the project you want to contribute to. Check out this guide by our Triage Team member, Christine Belzie, to learn more.

    Additional resources:

  • "Who's looking for open source contributors?"
  • Intro to Open Source Course

  1. My contribution does not show up on my OpenSauced profile. How do I fix it? Create an insight page or adding your merged pull request to the highlights page.

  2. I want to provide feedback on OpenSauced. We love feedback. Post your suggestion in our feedback repository.
